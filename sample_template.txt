Email Template & Guidelines for Theo Outreach

STRUCTURE:
1. Brief, respectful greeting
2. One sentence introduction of yourself as a student founder
3. One sentence about Theo and what it does
4. Specific pain point relevance to their school (reference their data)
5. Brief value proposition (how Theo helps)
6. Soft call-to-action (ask if they'd be interested in learning more)
7. Professional closing

TONE:
- Respectful and professional (you're a student reaching out to administrators)
- Humble and earnest, not salesy
- Evidence-based (reference their specific challenges)
- Concise (under 250 words)

EXAMPLE STRUCTURE:

Subject: Helping [School Name] with [Specific Pain Point]

Dear [Name],

I'm [Your Name], a student founder working on Theo, an AI teaching assistant designed specifically for educational institutions.

I noticed that [School Name] [specific pain point from data]. We've been working with similar schools to help address [related challenge] through personalized, agentic AI tutoring.

Theo provides 24/7 personalized support to students, helping reduce teacher workload while improving learning outcomes. Our platform adapts to each student's learning style and provides real-time insights to educators.

Given [tactical entry point], I thought you might be interested in learning how Theo could support [School Name]'s goals.

Would you be open to a brief conversation about how we might help?

Best regards,
[Your Name]
Founder, Theo
https://trytheo.org

KEY RULES:
- Never be pushy or demanding
- Don't oversell or make unrealistic promises
- Always reference specific data about their school
- Keep it under 250 words
- Maintain student founder authenticity
